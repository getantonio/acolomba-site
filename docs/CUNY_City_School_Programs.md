## CUNY Support Programs To Check

- CUNY ASAP: associate-degree support program for eligible students. Can include advising, tuition-gap help, textbook help, and transit support depending on eligibility and campus.
- CUNY ACE: bachelor's-degree support version at participating senior colleges.
- TAP / Pell / FAFSA: use grants first before loans.

## Timing Notes

- CUNY says Spring and Fall 2026 applications are currently open.
- Fall priority deadline is commonly February 1, but many campuses process later applications on a rolling basis.
- Spring priority deadline is commonly September 15.
- For Antonio, the fastest move is to apply for Fall 2026 now and ask each target school whether late-start Summer 2026 non-degree or certificate classes are still open.

## Staten Island / CSI Continuing Education

Status: strongest local fit if Antonio wants a short credential without starting another degree.

Updated preference: Antonio is most interested in art, AI, and anything Web3-adjacent. Because Antonio already has visual effects / 3D animation degrees, the strongest path is not "go back for another art degree." It is short AI/technology training plus portfolio pieces that show creative production, automation, and blockchain/Web3 awareness.

### Best-Fit CSI Options

1. CSI Online Technology Bootcamp - AI Agents & Workflow Automation
   - Best overall match.
   - 6 weeks online, live weekly sessions, non-credit certificate.
   - Posted tuition found: $1,656 paid in full.
   - Practical portfolio angle: build an AI agent that automates a creative workflow, such as image prompt intake, asset naming, client notes, shot review, or social content generation.

2. CSI Online Technology Bootcamp - AI Prompt Engineering
   - Best low-friction AI credential.
   - 6 weeks online, one live evening session per week.
   - Posted tuition found: $1,075 paid in full.
   - Practical portfolio angle: create a prompt system for concept art, visual direction, ad variations, product mockups, or AI video/image workflows.

3. CSI Online Technology Bootcamp - Data Analytics
   - Strong if the target is AI operations, creator analytics, marketing analytics, marketplace analytics, or NFT/Web3 community analytics.
   - 15 weeks online, live sessions twice per week.
   - Posted tuition found: $4,796 paid in full.
   - Practical portfolio angle: analyze marketplace/community/content data and turn it into a dashboard.

4. CSI Cloud Academy - AWS Cloud Practitioner
   - 3 months, 108 course hours, virtual.
   - Posted tuition found: $1,530, including registration fee, AWS Academy access, and one certification exam voucher.
   - Good technical support credential behind AI/web apps, media tooling, or Web3-adjacent cloud infrastructure.

5. CSI Art / Studio Art / Photography courses as a non-degree student
   - CSI offers Art BA/BFA paths, including Studio Art and Photography concentrations.
   - Since Antonio already has art degrees, use this only for selected credit-bearing courses, portfolio refresh, critique access, and structure.
   - Practical portfolio angle: produce a new AI-assisted visual series, product mockups, generative photography edits, or concept-art sequence.

6. CSI Media Culture / Communications: Design and Digital Media
   - Best CSI academic match for digital arts, graphic design, photo editing, layout, web design, media production, and social/media strategy.
   - Can be approached as non-degree coursework if available and prerequisites allow.
   - Practical portfolio angle: web presence, motion/short video, AI-assisted brand kit, landing pages, media campaign, or creator portfolio.

7. CSI Cinema Studies / production coursework
   - Good if the creative target is AI video, editing, motion, short-form content, or documentary/media production.
   - Practical portfolio angle: AI-assisted short video, shot breakdown, edit package, or production workflow.

8. Web3 / Blockchain at CSI
   - No strong CSI-specific continuing education Web3 certificate was found in this pass.
   - Treat Web3 as a portfolio layer on top of AI/art/cloud/data, not the main CSI credential.
   - Practical portfolio angle: generative art collection concept, wallet-gated portfolio demo, smart-contract explainer, token/NFT marketplace research brief, or simple Web3 front-end.

9. CSI Online Career Training - Entrepreneurship Start-Up Business Owner Management
   - Useful only if Antonio wants to package AI art / automation / Web3 services as a small business.

### Funding Notes For CSI Continuing Ed

- CSI Workforce Development continuing education is non-credit, so it does not accept traditional degree-program federal or state aid such as FAFSA, Pell, or TAP.
- CSI lists a Career Success Scholarship for Fall 2026 workforce training that can cover up to 90% of tuition, up to $3,500, for eligible applicants.
- The scholarship is competitive and does not cover fees, exams, or materials.
- CSI's page says ed2go online career programs are not eligible for Career Success Scholarship funding.
