# SUNY Program Options

Last updated: 2026-06-30

## Read

Summer 2026 is probably too tight for starting a full degree program, though late-start individual courses may still exist at some community colleges. Fall 2026 is the realistic target.

If Antonio is a New York resident age 25-55 with no completed college degree, SUNY Reconnect may be the strongest path because eligible associate degrees can cover tuition, fees, books, and supplies after FAFSA/TAP.

## Best First Targets

### 1. SUNY Reconnect Associate Degree

Status: Best value if eligible

Good-fit fields:

- Cybersecurity
- Computer information systems
- Networking and cybersecurity
- Technology
- Artificial intelligence, if an eligible program matches
- Supply chain/logistics, if aiming for operations jobs

Why this fits:

- Designed for adult learners.
- Can be full-time or part-time.
- Some programs are 100% online.
- May reduce or remove the need to use loans for tuition if eligible.

Eligibility to confirm:

- New York State resident or eligible for in-state tuition.
- Age 25-55.
- No existing college degree.
- Enrolled in an approved associate degree program.

### 2. Fully Online SUNY Associate Programs

Status: Strong fallback if Reconnect applies or if online flexibility matters most

Programs to inspect first:

- Finger Lakes Community College: Networking and Cybersecurity AAS, 100% online, 64 credits.
- Hudson Valley Community College: Computer Information Systems AAS/AS, 100% online.
- Genesee Community College: Cybersecurity, Systems and Networking AAS, 100% online.
- Herkimer / North Country: Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics AS options, 100% online.
- Monroe Community College: Business Administration AS, 100% online.

Why this fits:

- Builds a real credential while supporting immediate job-search positioning.
- Tech/support/cybersecurity tracks pair well with Antonio's current AI/workflow/operations direction.
- Business administration is safer if the goal is operations, coordination, support, or fintech-adjacent roles.

### 3. SUNY Empire State University

Status: Best bachelor's/credit-transfer route

Programs to inspect first:

- Business, Management and Economics BS/BBA
- Management BS
- Digital Media Arts BA/BS
- Digital Communication BS
- Science, Mathematics & Technology BA/BS

Why this fits:

- 100% online bachelor's options.
- Adult-friendly.
- Fall 2026 undergraduate application deadline found: August 7, 2026.
- Good if Antonio already has meaningful prior college credit.

### 4. NYC / Long Island Area SUNY Options

Status: Useful if in-person access matters

Campuses to inspect:

- Nassau Community College: Fall degree-seeking deadline found: August 13.
- Suffolk County Community College: encourages applying by April 1 for best selection, but should still be checked.
- Farmingdale State College: rolling admissions, but many transfer/fall deadlines cluster around July 1 and selective programs may be earlier.
- Westchester Community College: rolling admissions.
- FIT: worth checking only if visual/media/creative production is the target.

## Aid Notes

- File the 2026-27 FAFSA for Fall 2026 aid.
- If eligible for NY TAP, apply too; TAP does not have to be repaid.
- Federal Direct undergraduate loans for 2026-27 are fixed at 6.52% for subsidized/unsubsidized loans first disbursed July 1, 2026 through June 30, 2027.
- Loans must be repaid with interest, so use grants/Reconnect/TAP first.
- For federal student loans, degree-seeking enrollment and at least half-time enrollment are usually required; for undergraduates, half-time is commonly 6 credits.
