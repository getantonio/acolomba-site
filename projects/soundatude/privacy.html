Privacy Policy

Sound-a-tude is a simple background audio player for mindset loops and two-person conversation tracks.

Data Collection

The current app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Accounts

The current app does not use accounts or login.

Analytics and Tracking

The current app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.

Audio

Audio files are bundled with the app and play locally on the device. Local recordings, when used, stay on the device unless the user chooses to remove the app or its data.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed with the App Store release. The final public support email should be added before submission.

Changes

If the app later adds accounts, purchases, analytics, custom voice generation, cloud sync, or other data features, this policy should be updated before release.
