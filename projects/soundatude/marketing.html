Two-week rhythm

Let the loop become familiar enough to help.

  1. Days 1-3

    Get familiar. Notice which phrases feel true enough to repeat.

  2. Days 4-10

    Pair the loop with one small behavior: start, breathe, focus, greet, try again.

  3. Days 11-14

    Let the words remind you faster. Adjust the playlist to the lines that actually help.
