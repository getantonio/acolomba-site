A small personal sound instrument

One useful thought, practiced until it feels familiar.

Pick a phrase, choose an adult voice, keep the volume comfortable, and let the loop become a cue. Sound-a-tude keeps the controls close and the practice uncomplicated.

Voices and visual response

Hear the phrase. See its energy.

Choose from the adult voice library or use your own saved recording. The waveform responds to the source audio, while Volume changes the strength of the visual response as well as the sound.
