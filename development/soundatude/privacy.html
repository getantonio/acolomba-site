Privacy Policy

Sound-a-tude is a simple background audio player for mindset loops and two-person conversation tracks.

Data Collection

The current app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Accounts

The current app does not use accounts or login.

Analytics and Tracking

The current app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.

Audio

Audio files are bundled with the app and play locally on the device. Local recordings, when used, stay on the device unless the user chooses to remove the app or its data.

Subscriptions and Payments

Sound-a-tude offers an optional auto-renewable Plus subscription through Apple's App Store. Apple processes payments and maintains purchase records under the Apple Media Services Terms and Apple's privacy policy. The app reads StoreKit subscription status to unlock Plus features; Sound-a-tude does not receive or store payment card details.

Contact

For privacy questions, email getantonio@gmail.com.

Changes

This policy will be updated before release if the app adds accounts, analytics, custom voice generation, cloud sync, or other data features that change how information is handled.
