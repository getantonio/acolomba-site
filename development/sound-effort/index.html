The nightly rhythm

Not repair. Rehearsal.

Sound Effort is built around active identity practice: start the work, stay with the problem, accept mistakes, try again, and finish with pride.

  1. 01 Review

    What did I do today that made me stronger?

  2. 02 Choose

    What will I start early tomorrow?

  3. 03 Repeat

    A short loop turns effort into a voice they can remember.

Voices and avatars

A visible coach, with audio-only when needed.

The current plan is ElevenLabs for the Sound Effort voice pipeline, plus talking avatars for the Junior MVP. Start with stylized, bedtime-safe coaches rather than realistic child likenesses. Public child clips are reference-only. Audio-only mode stays available for bedtime, accessibility, and low-distraction listening.
