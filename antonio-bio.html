Antonio Colomba is a creative production tactician with professional work history in high-end photo retouching and undergraduate and master's degrees in 3D animation and visual effects. Much of that experience is being replaced by artificial intelligence today.

Background

Antonio studied animation and 3D visual effects, with interests in photography, lighting and design, in media production. His early work came through studios and agency environments where precision, quality control, following mark up, and calm communication mattered every day.

Antonio brings careful follow-through, visual judgment, patience under pressure, curiosity of emerging technology, and practical experience and know-how that helps work move forward.

Current Interests

His interests now extend into AI-assisted workflows, generative media, crypto and token platform experiments, trading tools, and practical project systems. The throughline is curiosity about how art, software, tools, and people fit together.

Personal Practice

Outside of work, Antonio is interested in running, martial arts, Muay Thai, and Brazilian jujitsu. These practices reflect a steady interest in discipline, repetition, pressure, resilience, and learning while supporting a physical and healthy lifestyle.
